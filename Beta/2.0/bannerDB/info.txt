?register - Register your account for pull

?pull [c/w/s] - Pull 10 in banner c - character, w - weapon, s -standard

?setpity [c/w/s] [Number] [y] - set custom pity for a specific banner. Put y if your next 5 star is guaranteed featured character

?summ - See your pull summary 

?reset - Reset your pull records

?abyss - See how much time left before abyss reset

?resin [Number1] [Number2] - See how much time your resin will be at Number2 (160 is default for Number2)
